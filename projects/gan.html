Image-based classification of Bangladeshi rice varieties using deep convolutional neural networks

  • Publisher: Springer Nature (Open Access)
  • Journal: Agricultural Products Processing and Storage (Vol. 2, Article 26, 2026)
  • Rice Varieties: Basmati, Chinigura, Jirashail, Kataribhog, Paijam
  • Models: VGG16, VGG19, ResNet50, Xception, DenseNet + custom CNN (BDriceNetworkV0)
  • Tech Stack: Python, TensorFlow/Keras, PyTorch, OpenCV, NumPy, Matplotlib, scikit-learn
  • DOI: 10.1007/s44462-026-00061-9
  • Paper (PDF): Download

In this Springer Nature open-access research article, we benchmarked deep convolutional neural networks for fine-grained classification of 5 popular Bangladeshi rice varieties. We curated a balanced dataset of 15,000 high-quality grain images captured under controlled conditions and evaluated multiple pretrained CNN architectures alongside a lightweight custom model (BDriceNetworkV0). VGG19 achieved the best overall performance (99.73% test accuracy), while the custom model reached 98.60% accuracy with lower computational costâ€”supporting potential deployment in resource-constrained settings.
